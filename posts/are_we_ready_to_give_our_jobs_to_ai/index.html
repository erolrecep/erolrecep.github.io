This article is about my views on ai tools and software engineering jobs are being replaced by ai tools or build teams with hybrid approach. _ There are number of instances that shows that Google, X, Salesforce etc prefer to switch to coding assistant plus experienced human combined software development teams. _ Who is going to hire the inexperienced developers/software engineers/ml engineers/data scientist? _ The quality of the coding assistance change based on the knowledge of prompt engineering, engineer’s technical knowledge, the flow of software development and so on.
